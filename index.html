Christmas Trees: A Holiday Tradition

Christmas trees are one of the most beloved holiday traditions, bringing joy and festive spirit to homes around the world.

Popular Christmas Tree Varieties

  • Balsam Fir - Known for its dark-green needles and wonderful aroma
  • Fraser Fir - Popular for its excellent needle retention and strong branches
  • Blue Spruce - Distinctive blue-gray color and perfect symmetrical shape
  • Scotch Pine - Classic Christmas tree with excellent needle retention

Tree Decorating Tips

Follow these steps for a beautifully decorated tree:

  1. Start with lights, working from the inside out
  2. Add your main garland or ribbon
  3. Hang larger ornaments first, spacing them evenly
  4. Fill in with smaller ornaments and specialty pieces
  5. Finish with tinsel or icicles for extra sparkle

Holiday Care Tip: Keep your tree fresh throughout the season by ensuring it always has plenty of water. A fresh tree can drink up to a gallon of water in the first 24 hours!
